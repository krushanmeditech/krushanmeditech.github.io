About Us

A Trusted Partner in Healthcare Infrastructure

Krushan Meditech Private Limited is a MedTech enterprise headquartered in Pune and Mumbai, operating within a group that includes Heureka Medihealth, Credknow Solutions, and Vimal Enterprises. We bridge the gap between advanced global technology and India's growing public healthcare system.

Government & Institutional Focus
Deep expertise in PM-JAY, ESIS, CGMSCL, and state-level procurement with compliant documentation and empanelment support.
End-to-End Solutions
From procurement and installation to commissioning, training, and ongoing service support across all supplied technologies.
Patient-First Impact
Every deployment is designed to improve clinical outcomes, reduce costs for patients, and expand access to advanced care.
